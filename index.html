📕 Hostel Rules & Regulations

Official rules applicable to all hostel residents

📜 Rules & Regulations

  1. Breakfast Hours: Breakfast is served from 8:30 AM to 9:30 AM.
  2. Hostel Time: Students must leave for hostel daily before 9:30 AM to go for College.
  3. Lunch Hours: Lunch is served from 1:00 PM to 2:00 PM.
  4. Mobile Phone Policy: The use of mobile phones is strictly prohibited within the hostel.
  5. Dining Policy: Only hostel-provided food may be consumed while staying on the premises.
  6. Room Discipline: Students are permitted only in their assigned rooms; visiting other rooms is strictly forbidden.
  7. Curfew: The hostel gate will be closed promptly at 5:30 PM every day.
  8. Dinner Time: Dinner is served at 7:00 PM.
  9. Study Hours: Mandatory study time is observed from 8:00 PM to 10:00 PM.
  10. Weekly Meeting: A mandatory meeting with the Warden will be conducted every Tuesday from 6:00 PM to 7:00 PM.
